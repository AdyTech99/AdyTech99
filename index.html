Farlands reborn

This mod brings back the farlands in modern Minecraft versions. Version 1.19+. The Farland Reborn Minecraft mod is a modification for Minecraft that reintroduces the "Far Lands" phenomenon that was present in older versions of the game. The Far Lands were a series of terrain generation anomalies that occurred at extreme distances from the world's center in versions of Minecraft prior to Beta 1.8. These anomalies created bizarre and fascinating landscape formations due to floating-point precision errors in the terrain generation code.
